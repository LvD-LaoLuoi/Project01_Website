Decription:

Visitors to Khuon Than can walk on the lake by boat and visit the lush green orchards as far as the eye can see. This is also the place to reside, nurture and preserve the unique cultural values of ethnic minorities such as the San Chi, Cao Lan, Tay, and Nung with their love folk songs... After a while Traveling on a boat, visitors will have the opportunity to enjoy the sweet taste of specialties: honey, chameleon wine, chestnuts, lychee, persimmon, na ... of ethnic minorities such as Kinh, Cao Lan , Tay, San Chi, Nung. Especially the fish dish. Anyone who has enjoyed the grilled goby dish on the ethnic minorities' charcoal stove will never forget it.
